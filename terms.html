By uploading or entering student information through a teacher or school professional account, you represent and warrant that:

  • You have appropriate authorization from your school or district to share student information with FairGame for science fair program delivery purposes
  • Where required by your school or district policy, you have obtained or facilitated parental consent for the sharing of student information
