Support Our Work

Fund a science fair program.

Every dollar goes directly toward getting a school's science fair off the ground. FairGame is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit — all donations are tax-deductible.

Your Impact

What your donation funds

$25
Covers display board materials for one student who can't afford them
$100
Pays for OAS district registration for a school with limited budget
$250
Funds a full classroom workshop session for 30 students
$500
Covers trophies and recognition materials for a school fair
$1,000
Sponsors one complete school fair from planning to awards day
$5,000
Funds an entire school's program for a full academic year

FairGame operates with minimal overhead — we are a volunteer-led organization and we keep administrative costs below 10% of all donations. The majority of every dollar reaches students and schools directly.
