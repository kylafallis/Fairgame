From first project to internationals

"
I genuinely know that I wouldn't have made it to ISEF without Kyla. She read through everything and answered all my questions. I think one time we were on a Zoom call for two hours. But now I'm going to internationals!
Rishi Pampati
Walnut Hills High School, Cincinnati, Ohio

How Rishi got to ISEF

Rishi came to FairGame as a student at Walnut Hills High School looking for guidance on his research project. He had a strong idea but needed support navigating the science fair process - from experimental design through the competition pathway.

Through FairGame's individual mentorship program, Rishi was connected with Kyla Fallis, FairGame's founder and a multi-award-winning science fair competitor herself (ISEF qualifier, EPA Award recipient). Over several months and many hours of calls and document reviews, Rishi developed and refined his project.

Rishi advanced from his school fair through district and state competitions, ultimately qualifying for Regeneron ISEF - the world's largest pre-college science competition.
