Welcome to FairGame, student!

This is your personal hub — track your science fair project, access exclusive resources, connect with the FairGame community, and get support from our team whenever you need it.

Getting Started

1
Set up your project
Enter your project title, field, and a brief description so FairGame can connect you with the right resources and mentors.
2
Download your resources
Grab the Research Guide, Display Board Template, and Project Proposal form from the Resources section.
3
Join the community
Connect with other students, find a mentor, and stay up to date on FairGame events and deadlines.
4
Reach out for help
Have questions? Use the Ask for Help section to message the FairGame team or schedule a call.
