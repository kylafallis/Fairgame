How to approve a user

1. Click "Approve" to mark their request as active.
2. Go to Supabase → Authentication → Users → Invite User.
3. Enter their email and set user metadata: {"role": "teacher", "name": "Their Name"}
4. They receive a sign-in link. Once they log in, they land on their role dashboard automatically.
